No Need For Crabs!

In the late 90s, Cartoon Network started showing several different anime shows. One of them, Tenchi Muyo, really resonated with me. I thought it was really funny and I liked the characters.


During one of the episodes, Hello Baby!, the super-genius Washu is pondering for a while and allows her computer interface's screen saver to activate. The screen saver, though only seen for a few seconds (as far as I am aware, never again in any other episode or movie) is a pair of simple red crabs walking across the screen.


Ever since I saw that episode I always thought it would be neat to have that for a real screen saver.

  • Mac OS X

Most recently, I have ported it to run as webGL and you can see it as the background of this page!
